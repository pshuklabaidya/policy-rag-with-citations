# Expense Reimbursement Policy

Synthetic document for portfolio demonstration only.

## EX-1 Eligible Expenses

Employees may request reimbursement for reasonable business expenses, including approved travel, lodging, meals, software subscriptions, conference fees, and client-related expenses.

## EX-2 Receipts

Receipts are required for all expenses of $25 or more. Receipts must show the vendor, date, amount, and business purpose.

## EX-3 Submission Deadline

Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the transaction date. Late submissions require manager approval and may be denied if business justification is missing.

## EX-4 Meal Limits

Meal reimbursement is limited to $25 for breakfast, $35 for lunch, and $60 for dinner, unless a higher amount is approved for client entertainment or executive travel.

## EX-5 Prohibited Expenses

The company does not reimburse personal entertainment, commuting costs, fines, penalties, personal subscriptions, gifts over approved limits, or expenses lacking a business purpose.

## EX-6 Approval

Managers must review expense reports for business purpose, policy compliance, receipt completeness, and budget availability before approval.
