# Remote Work Policy

Synthetic document for portfolio demonstration only.

## RW-1 Eligibility

Full-time employees may request remote work eligibility after 90 days of employment. Approval depends on role suitability, performance standing, manager approval, and business requirements.

## RW-2 Work Location

Employees approved for remote work must maintain a reliable work location with stable internet access, reasonable privacy, and the ability to join video meetings when required.

## RW-3 Core Collaboration Hours

Remote employees must be available during core collaboration hours from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their assigned team time zone, Monday through Friday, unless otherwise approved.

## RW-4 Equipment

Company-issued laptops, monitors, and security accessories must be used for regular remote work. Personal devices may not be used for company systems unless explicitly approved by IT.

## RW-5 Travel To Office

Remote employees may be required to attend quarterly planning sessions, team offsites, onboarding events, or critical business meetings in person.

## RW-6 Policy Violations

Failure to follow remote work requirements may result in revocation of remote work privileges, disciplinary review, or additional security controls.
